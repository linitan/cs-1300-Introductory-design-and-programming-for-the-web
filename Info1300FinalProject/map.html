How to get to the Commons:

For GPS, use the street address at 171 E M.L.K. Jr. St, Ithaca, NY 14850

Finding a parking space:

Park your cars at Green Street Garage(bottom marker)

For GPS, use the street address at 120-126 E Green St, Ithaca, NY 14850

Using TCAT from Cornell University:

Option 1: Board the Route 30 at Risley Hall(Cornell North Campus) and get off at Seneca @ Commons

Option 2: Board the Route 30 at Sage Hall(Cornell Central Campus) and get off at Seneca @ Commons

Option 3: Board the Route 70 or 72 at Anabel Taylor Hall(Cornell Central Campus) and get off at Seneca @ Commons
